General Information

  • Blood pressure tests are usually free.
  • Cholesterol tests usually cost about $35. Be sure to request a Full Lipid Panel that includes: Total Cholesterol, HDL, and LDL.
  • For best results, do not eat 9-12 hours before a cholesterol test.
